How Can I Upgrade My Netflix Plan to stream on 2 screens?


  1. Go to netflix official website - Netflix.com/tv2. Tap Login button and enter Gmail & Password.
  2. Click on "Change Plan" under "Account" section. Select Standard plan.
  3. Complete Payment steps.
  4. Open Netflix App on your TV. After you login account an activation code appears.
  5. Now, on Netflix.com/tv2 activation page, enter the 6-digits Activation code shown on your TV screen.
  6. Follow on-screen instructions to complete Standard Plan activation.
  7. Begin streaming Netflix on 2 TV screens simultaneously.
